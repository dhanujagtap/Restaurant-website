Desi Donne Biryani is ancient and authentic. We always go the extra mile to satisfy food lovers from all corners of the world. And so, we have taken our classic donne biryani and experimented with Telangana and Andhra flavours for a regional touch and a few modern flavours to give it a modern and contemporary twist all the while carefully retaining the authentic taste.Having said about the authenticity in taste, we source our ingredients grown with love and compassion directly from the smallholder farmers in the far off villages creating a meaningful impact for all.
